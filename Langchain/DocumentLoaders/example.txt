A quiet breeze crossed the hill one night,
Carrying whispers wrapped in moonlight.
The stars leaned low to hear the trees,
Singing old songs with effortless ease.

A river wandered, calm and slow,
Reflecting dreams from worlds below.
Each ripple held a fleeting spark,
A tiny lantern in the dark.

The mountains stood in patient grace,
Guarding time in their embrace.
While clouds drifted like thoughts set free,
Across the endless silver sea.

And somewhere far, yet close somehow,
The future rested in the now.
A gentle reminder, soft and true—
The world keeps blooming around you.
